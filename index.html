About

I am a graduate student in the Computer Science department at KAUST where I work at SANDS Lab under the supervision of Prof. Marco Canini.
My research at KAUST focuses on distributed optimization.
Previously, I completed my undergraduate at IIT Kanpur with a major in Mathematics & Scientic Computing and minors in Algorithms and Machine Learning.
