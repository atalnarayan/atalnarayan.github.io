About

I work as a Software Engineer at PrashantAdvait Foundation.
In a past life, I was a graduate student in the Computer Science department at KAUST where I worked at SANDS Lab under the supervision of Prof. Marco Canini. My research at KAUST focused on distributed optimization. Even before that, I completed my undergraduate at IIT Kanpur with a major in Mathematics & Scientic Computing and minors in Algorithms and Machine Learning.
